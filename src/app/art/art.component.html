My Art

Jules Crystal Design is creating unique jewelry that brings you a full magical experience. The pieces are inspired from far away realms, mythical creatures and forgotten fairytales. All of them are created by hand in different styles in the heart of Transylvania.

The techniques used in the process of creation are various. This way, we always bring new pieces with character and special features.

Jules Crystal Design is my vision of breaking free from all the ordinary daily jewelry and seeking the paramountey. I am dreaming my creation and then I create my dream.

Collections

I first launched a collection in October 2019. It was the moment I decided to take my jewelry pieces to the next level. I was enchanted by the way jewelry collections looked with all the pieces that were from the same stories with vibrant colors that complete each other. Since then, I launched 4 collections.

1. Fantasy Fall Collection

My first Collection launched in October 2019 was also the first time I tried sculpting jewelry for the first time. This collection had its inspiration from Fantasy series as it already says in the title. I created unique pendants with stories behind them. Each and every piece of this collection has its own story of inspiration that stronlgy influences the design . And as the first collection of Jules Crystal Design, I wanted it to be special so I picked a series of unique, high-quality stones. The collection was a complete success. In only a few days, it was almost sold out!

2. Elderwood Collection

Elderwood Collection, also my second collection was launched in November the same year. With the aproach of the cold Winter, I wanted to bring people the raw green of the plants one more. So I launched this collection. It had green elements in almost every piece. The little nature symbols didn’t miss from this one neither. I wanted it to speak for itself, right to the customer’s heart.

3. Celestial Collection

This is by far the most colorful collection I ever had. It was inspired from the worlds above, from sunset lands no one had seen. It had plenty of the colors you see in the infinite universe with beautiful shining stars and burning suns. Many of the pieces were sculpted and painted by hand.

4. Ice Queen Collection

And last but not least, my last collection which also happens to be my favorite collection. Ice Queen was launched in February 2020. This one was made to make my beautiful customers feel like their inner queens. It had a beautiful combination between white and blue icy stones that bring the cold outside. The elegance can be seen from far away. It was an important aspect of this collection.

Custom Orders

You dream it and we create it

I usually take custom orders. Below there is a picture with one of my recent customs. You can reach me out on Instagram, email or even online shop. You can tell me how you visualize your jewelry, we can further discuss it together and find a way to make a great piece that fits you. You can express your ideas in any way you feel is most useful regarding your dream jewelry piece. It can be through a text, an image, a voice message, sketch, whatever you think helps you the most. I am open to anything. The prices will be set before I start working considering the amount of work, materials and difficulty. After everything is set, the customer will have to pay in advance 50% on the item’s full price through PayPal or Bank Transfer. The full price will be payed after the item is finished either when the item is shipped or before through Bank Transfer or PayPal.
