Inside Jules Crystal Design

My name is Iulia and I am the creator of what you see. Everything started in Romania, a small counrty from Eastern Europe, also known as the country of Trasnylvania. I am currently an Interior Architecture Design student at "Ion Mincu" University of Architecture from Bucharest. I am a lover of nature and my world revolves around mountain hikes, architecture and jewelry . I started this handmade journey 5 years ago when I saw my mom creating rustic wood painted necklaces and I decided to give it a try.

In the beginning I was really young and clueless therefore I had so many fails.But after a few long years of investing and working hard I started improving. I finally created my Instagram account and opened my Breslo shop in October 2018. And since then people started to get to know my jewelry and my community is growing slowly but surely. I started taking professional photos of my art, investing more on quality materials and letting my whole creativity out. At the beginning of 2020 I started my Etsy shop and I am so happy that I can now ship my art across the globe.

Jules Crystal Design is my vision of breaking free from all the ordinary daily jewelry and seeking the paramountey. I am dreaming my creation and then I create my dream.

Since I love my pieces to be one of a kind, I spend hours seeking high-quality materials and look for unique and beautiful stones and crystals. I almost never buy them online because I like to hand-pick them so they have the most awesome colors and shapes.

As a small business owner, I design and sketch my jewelry, create them, take the pictures, model them, edit the photos, maintain the online shops and the social media, handle the orders, pack them up and ship them out. There is a lot of work behind a handmade artist and by buying a handmade item you not only get a pretty thing, but you also support a hardworking artist and also a dream. And another good feature about handmade brands is that you know the person who creates your item, the true process and any other information as materials or other details whereas at big companies we all have no clue about the environment they make their products or who is behind it.
