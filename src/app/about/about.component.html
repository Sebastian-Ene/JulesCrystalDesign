Get to know us

My name is Iulia and I am the creator of what you see. I am currently an Interior Architecture Designer at "Ion Mincu" University of Architecture from Bucharest. I am a lover of nature and my world revolves around mountain hikes, architecture and jewelry . I started this handmade journey 5 years ago when I saw my mom creating rustic wood painted necklaces.

In the beginning I was really young and clueless therefore I had so many fails. Until October 2018 all I did was invest time and money while having almost no orders. Then I finally started my Instagram account and opened my Breslo shop. And since then people started to get to know my jewelry and my community is growing slowly but surely. At the beginning of 2020 I started my Etsy shop and I am so happy that I can now ship my art across the globe.
